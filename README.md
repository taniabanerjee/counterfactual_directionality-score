# Digital Twin in Spatial Transcriptomics

This repository hosts research, publications, software, and supplementary materials related to the development of digital twin models for spatial transcriptomics.

Our research aims to develop computational frameworks that integrate spatial organization, cellular interactions, regulatory programs, and perturbational analysis to better understand tissue ecosystems and disease progression.

## Research Themes

* Cell–cell communication and directional influence
* Counterfactual analysis in spatial transcriptomics
* Spatial graph learning
* Digital twin modeling of tissue microenvironments
* Tumor–immune interactions
* Gene regulatory network modeling
* Explainable and interpretable spatial AI
